Frequently Asked

Questions Answered.

You can expect 50-70 km per charge depending on terrain, rider weight, and assist level used. Flat roads with moderate pedalling and medium assist will give you the upper end of that range.
Approximately 4 hours for a full charge using the standard charger included with every bike. The battery is removable — you can bring it inside your home or office to charge without moving the bike.
The battery carries a 1-year warranty. The motor and electrical system are covered separately. Ezee Cycles also provides lifetime service support at nominal cost for all models.
Every Ezee Cycles bike — Orca or Pace — includes a charger, tool kit, front and rear mudguards, pillion foot rest, heavy-duty side stand, and a rear carrier. All of these are included in the listed price at no extra charge.
Yes, EMI financing is available. Options and eligibility depend on your profile and preferred financier. Contact us on WhatsApp or through the form and we will share current EMI plans available in Bangalore.
Yes. Standard colors — Black and White — are ready to ship. Custom colors are available at an additional cost depending on your requirements. The process involves a WhatsApp consultation with our design team to discuss and finalize before production begins.
The Orca is the premium model with a larger 15Ah battery and — in the Display variant — a digital dashboard. The Pace is the practical value option with a 13Ah battery at a significantly lower price point. Both include the same free accessories and core features.
One key locks and unlocks the battery for removal. The second key controls the motor and electrical system. This means even if someone removes the battery key, the motor remains locked — adding a second layer of security.
Yes. There is a USB charging port directly on the battery that works while the bike is in use. You can charge a phone, earphones, or any small USB-powered device during your ride.
